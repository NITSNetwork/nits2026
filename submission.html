Abstract Submission

NITS welcomes contributions on all aspects related to Translation and Interpreting Studies in the broadest sense. Submissions can take the form of:

  • 15-minute paper presentations on educational or research projects (current and/or future)
  • posters related to educational or research projects (current and/or future)

We welcome contributions from (early-career) researchers as well as MA and PhD students.

Abstracts should contain a maximum of 300 words (excl. references). They should clearly indicate the type of submission (paper or poster) and can be written in Dutch or English.
