Directions
From Brussels Central Station, take bus line 71 direction Delta to ULB.
From nearby hotels, take tram line 8 direction Roodebeek to ULB.

For single journeys, you can pay contactless with capped fares directly in the tram or bus.
For multiple journeys, you can buy a Brupass 10 card at a vending machine in most stops.
